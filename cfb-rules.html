EA Sports College Football Online Dynasty Rules

Our league prioritizes realistic, sim-style gameplay. Rules may be updated at any time if issues arise that impact fair play and competition.

Overview

  • Platform: PlayStation 5
  • Game: EA Sports College Football 26
  • League Size: 32 users
  • Conferences: ACC, Big Ten, Big 12, SEC (6–8 users in each)
  • Custom Coaches Only (no default coaches)
  • Custom schedules + coaching carousel enabled
  • Custom playbooks allowed and encouraged
  • Soft advance every 72 hours at 8PM ET
  • Hybrid 48-hour advance applies when:
    • All User vs User games are completed
    • No one has requested extra time

Gameplay Settings

  • Offensive Skill: All-American
  • Defensive Skill: All-American
  • Quarter Length: 12 Minutes
  • Clock Runoff: 18 seconds
  • Game Speed: Normal
  • Home Field Advantage: On
  • Ballhawk: Off
  • Heat Seeker: Off
  • Switch Assist: User choice
  • Sliders: Custom by Mike Lowe

Sim Gameplay Expectations

Violations of sim rules may result in removal from the league.

Kneeling / End-Game Situations

If the CPU is not calling timeouts and the game is decided, you are expected to kneel out the clock.

Stat Padding

No force-feeding stats. All games are streamed and stat padding is obvious.

Chew Clock

  • No Chew Clock until the 4th quarter.
  • Losing team can use Chew Clock early to finish the game; winning team may match it.

Unstoppable / Glitch Plays

No repeated exploit plays, motion glitches, nano-blitz spam, etc. Use realistic football concepts.

Flipping Plays

Do not use the in-play flip mechanic. Call an audible instead.

Player Movement

  • No zig-zag running to manipulate pursuit.
  • No sprinting directly to sidelines on returns.
  • Normal jukes/spins are allowed.

QB Movement

  • Remain in the pocket unless pressured.
  • No sprinting drop-backs 10+ yards.
  • No auto-sideline sprints after snap.
  • Designated read options are allowed but must be used in reasonable moderation.
  • QB Playmaker (Right Stick WR Direction) is banned.

WR Control

You may not user-control WR routes or adjust catch animations. Let ratings play.

Hot Routes

  • Limit: 1 hot route per play (not counting blocking adjustments).
  • No streak/fade hot routes from compressed sets.

No Huddle

  • Max: One back-to-back no-huddle (play must be changed).
  • In final 2 minutes (or down big), up to 3 consecutive no-huddles allowed.

Snap Timing

Wait **3 seconds** after players are set before snapping (except spike situations).

Defense

  • Must rush at least 3 players.
  • Grounded switching: Only switch after catch or on run plays.
  • Pre-snap: You may manually move only one defender.
  • Tackling: Hit stick / cut stick should be realistic, not spammed.

Special Teams

  • No more than 1 fake kick per game.
  • No manually pulling blockers back on returns.

4th Down Rules

Follow ESPN Analytics 4th down chart except when:

  • AI coach suggests going for it
  • You are losing in the 4th quarter

Participation & Streaming Requirements

  • All games must be streamed and linked in Discord before kickoff.
  • Archives must be enabled.
  • Missing multiple CPU games or ghosting opponents may lead to removal.

Coaching & Position Rules

  • Position changes must follow weight/speed requirements.
  • No converting players just to gain badges.
  • Ask an admin before making any questionable position move.

Punishments

  • 1st Violation: Punt on 1st down + suspend top 2 players next User game.
  • 2nd Violation: Removal from league.
